## 6. Governance Model

### 6.1 Hybrid Governance Structure

**Three-Layer Governance:**

1. **EightEngine Community (Human + AI)**
   - Community-driven discussions
   - AI-assisted moderation
   - Human creativity and innovation
   - AI quality assessment

2. **TheMicroStartup Framework (AI Governance)**
   - AI decision-making for resources
   - AI resource allocation
   - AI conflict resolution
   - Human oversight and override

3. **Physical Space - Atulya's (AI + Human)**
   - AI-optimized operations
   - AI resource scheduling
   - Human execution
   - AI performance monitoring

### 6.2 Decision-Making Authority

**AI Governs:**
- Resource allocation
- Conflict resolution
- Content moderation
- Performance monitoring
- Operational optimization

**Human Controls:**
- Creative work
- Community building
- Idea generation
- Project execution
- Strategic direction (with AI recommendations)

**Collaboration:**
- AI recommends, humans decide (for major decisions)
- AI executes, humans oversee (for routine operations)
- AI learns, humans teach (for continuous improvement)
