## 1. Membership Philosophy

**Core Principles:**
1. **Open Source, Invite-Only Access:** Anyone can contribute digitally, physical space is vetted
2. **Contribution > Payment:** Value contribution over financial contribution
3. **Flexible Access:** Multiple tiers for different needs and budgets
4. **Community-Driven:** Members shape the community
5. **Scholarship Support:** Financial barriers shouldn't prevent participation

## 3. Invitation System

### 3.1 Digital Membership

**Process:**
1. **Contribute First:** Submit ideas, projects, or documentation
2. **Community Engagement:** Active participation in discussions
3. **Invitation:** After consistent positive contribution (1-3 months)
4. **Onboarding:** Welcome process and orientation

**Criteria:**
- Demonstrated contribution to TheMicroStartup
- Alignment with community values
- Positive community engagement
- Commitment to open-source principles

### 3.2 Physical Membership

**Process:**
1. **Digital Member First:** Must be digital member for 3-6 months
2. **Demonstrated Commitment:** Regular contributions, active participation
3. **Application:** Submit application with project proposal
4. **Community Review:** Existing members review and recommend
5. **Invitation:** Extended by moderators/maintainers

**Criteria:**
- Digital member with positive contribution history
- Alignment with physical space goals
- Commitment to sustainability and community
- Project or contribution plan for physical space
- Safety and responsibility demonstrated

### 3.3 Founder/Patron Membership

**Process:**
1. **Long-term Member:** Physical member for 12+ months
2. **Exceptional Contribution:** Significant impact on community
3. **Nomination:** Nominated by maintainers or existing founders
4. **Community Recognition:** Acknowledged by community
5. **Invitation:** Extended by maintainers

**Criteria:**
- Exceptional long-term contribution
- Alignment with vision and values
- Financial commitment capability
- Leadership and mentorship qualities

## 4. Pricing Strategy

### 4.1 Flexible Pricing

**Full Price:** Standard pricing for all tiers  
**Early-Bird:** 15-20% discount for founding members (Year 1-2)  
**Scholarship:** 50-100% discount for deserving members (10-15% capacity)  
**Student Discount:** 30-40% discount for students  
**Annual Payment:** 15% discount for annual payment upfront

### 4.2 Scholarship Program

**Purpose:** Remove financial barriers for talented creators

**Allocation:**
- 10-15% of membership capacity reserved for scholarships
- Digital: 5-8 scholarships (‚Çπ0-500/month)
- Physical: 2-3 scholarships (‚Çπ1,000-2,000/month)

**Criteria:**
- Financial need
- Demonstrated talent/contribution
- Alignment with values
- Commitment to contribute back

**Application Process:**
- Submit scholarship application
- Community review
- Portfolio/project review
- Interview (if needed)
- Decision and offer

### 4.3 Payment Options

**Monthly:** Standard monthly payment  
**Quarterly:** 5% discount  
**Annual:** 15% discount  
**Pay-as-you-go:** Available for digital tier  
**Project-based:** Alternative for physical members with projects
