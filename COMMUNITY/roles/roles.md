# Community Roles

**TheMicroStartup** operates on a **zero hierarchy** model with clear roles that focus on **contribution over title**.

---

## Role Philosophy

- **Everyone is a creator** - No passive consumers
- **Roles are fluid** - People can take on different roles for different projects
- **Contribution defines status** - Your work matters, not your title
- **Mentorship over management** - We guide and support, not command and control

## Role Transitions

### Growing into Roles

1. **Start as Contributor** - Just contribute!
2. **Demonstrate Consistency** - Regular, quality contributions
3. **Take Initiative** - Start projects or organize working groups
4. **Show Leadership** - Help others, resolve conflicts, share knowledge
5. **Earn Trust** - Community recognizes your contributions

### Stepping Down

- Anyone can step down from a role at any time
- Transition smoothly by documenting knowledge
- Hand off responsibilities to others
- Stay connected as a contributor

## Decision Making

### Project-Level Decisions
- Made by **Project Leads**
- Documented in project README or docs
- Community can provide input through issues/discussions

### Domain-Level Decisions
- Made by **Working Group Coordinators**
- Input from domain contributors
- Documented in domain docs

### Community-Level Decisions
- Made by **Maintainers** with community input
- Documented in `COMMUNITY/meeting-notes/`
- Major decisions go through community discussion

### How to Propose Changes

1. Open an issue or discussion
2. Tag relevant working groups
3. Gather feedback
4. Present proposal
5. Document decision
