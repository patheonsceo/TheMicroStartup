## 5. Community Structure

### 5.1 Membership Levels

**Public Member (EightEngine):**
- Free access to EightEngine
- Can submit ideas and projects
- Can participate in discussions
- Can attend public events
- Can contribute to community

**Digital Member (TheMicroStartup):**
- Invited from EightEngine (after contribution)
- Enhanced access to TheMicroStartup resources
- Discounted workshops and events
- Project collaboration opportunities

**Physical Member (Atulya's):**
- Invited from active contributors
- Physical space access
- Lab access and accommodation
- Extended community benefits

### 5.2 Community Roles

**Community Roles:**
- **Public Members:** Everyone in EightEngine
- **Active Contributors:** Regular contributors
- **Moderators:** Community moderators (human + AI-assisted)
- **Maintainers:** TheMicroStartup maintainers

## 8. Community Guidelines

### 8.1 EightEngine Values

**Core Values (Aligned with TheMicroStartup):**
- **Open Source, Not Free Ride:** Every member contributes value
- **Self-Sustainability First:** Financial + ecological + emotional balance
- **Maker > Consumer:** Everyone builds something
- **Fun is Serious:** Space for play, curiosity, and experimentation
- **Respect & Play:** No toxicity, pressure, or commercial greed

### 8.2 Code of Conduct

**EightEngine follows TheMicroStartup Code of Conduct:**
- See [COMMUNITY/code-of-conduct.md](code-of-conduct.md)
- Applied across both platforms
- AI and human enforcement
- Transparent moderation

### 8.3 Community Rules

1. **Contribute Meaningfully:** Every member contributes value
2. **Respect Community:** Follow code of conduct
3. **Build, Don't Destroy:** Focus on creation, not criticism
4. **Share Knowledge:** Open knowledge sharing
5. **AI Decisions:** Trust but verify AI decisions
