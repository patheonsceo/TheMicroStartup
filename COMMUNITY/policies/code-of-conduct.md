# Code of Conduct

**TheMicroStartup** is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors and community members.

---

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Education
- Socio-economic status
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Encouraged Behavior

- **Respectful Communication**
  - Use welcoming and inclusive language
  - Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
  - Gracefully accept constructive criticism
  - Focus on what is best for the community

- **Collaborative Spirit**
  - Show empathy towards other community members
  - Celebrate successes together
  - Support each other's learning and growth
  - Share knowledge openly

- **Constructive Contribution**
  - Provide helpful feedback
  - Acknowledge mistakes and learn from them
  - Be open to different approaches
  - Focus on solutions, not problems

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory remarks
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document the incident**
   - Note what happened, when, and where
   - Save any relevant messages or communications

2. **Report to moderators**
   - Email: [coming soon]
   - Discord/Telegram: Contact moderators directly
   - GitHub: Open an issue (private if needed)

3. **Confidentiality**
   - Reports will be handled confidentially
   - We respect the privacy of all parties involved

---

## Enforcement

Community moderators are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. They will:

- Investigate reports promptly and fairly
- Provide clear explanations for any actions taken
- Maintain confidentiality when appropriate
- Work to resolve conflicts constructively

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** - A private warning about behavior
2. **Temporary Ban** - Temporary exclusion from community spaces
3. **Permanent Ban** - Permanent exclusion for severe or repeated violations

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community spaces (online and offline)
- All community events
- All official communication channels
- All project-related activities
