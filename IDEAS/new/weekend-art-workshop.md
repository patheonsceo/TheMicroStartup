## Problem Statement

Many people want to learn art but don't know where to start. Weekend workshops provide an opportunity to learn, create, and connect with others.

---

## Proposed Solution

Run 1-2 art workshops per month on Saturdays. Each workshop lasts 3-4 hours. Focus on one skill per workshop (like basic drawing, watercolor painting, or digital art basics). Keep it simple and beginner-friendly.

3. Create simple workshop schedule
4. Set up art lab space with tables and supplies

6. Collect registration and payments
7. Run first workshop
8. Get feedback and improve
