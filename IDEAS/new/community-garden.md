## Overview

Create a small garden at Atulya's where members can grow vegetables, herbs, and fruits. This provides fresh food for the kitchen and teaches people about growing food.

---

## Problem Statement

Buying vegetables and herbs from markets is expensive. Growing our own food would reduce costs, provide fresh ingredients, and create a learning experience for everyone.

---

## Proposed Solution

Set aside an area (maybe 500-800 square feet) for a garden. Start with easy-to-grow items like tomatoes, okra, spinach, mint, and coriander. Use composting from kitchen waste as fertilizer.

---

## Implementation Plan

1. Choose a good spot with enough sunlight
2. Prepare the soil (remove weeds, add compost)
3. Build small raised beds or plant directly in ground
4. Plant seeds or small plants
5. Create watering schedule
6. Teach members how to maintain it
